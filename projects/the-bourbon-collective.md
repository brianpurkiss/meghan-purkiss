description: |
  Logo Design

  This logo was designed for a local bourbon bar in Cedar Park, TX. The Bourbon Collective will be a high-end, luxurious bar that exclusively serves the best bourbons. The client wanted the logo to reflect this high-end luxurious vibe of The Bourbon Collective.

  I designed this logo using a beautiful serif font. I then spaced out the kerning of the letters for a clean, airy feel. The beautifully simplistic grain icon symbolizes the grains that are used to make bourbon. To add to the lux design style of the logo, I added shapes around the text and icon to really make this logo stand out. Encasing the text and icon with the rectangle and triangular shapes, elevates the design from a standard wordmark, into a polished logo design.
